
---
title: RPO vs In‑House Hiring — When Each Model Wins
date: 2025-10-20
category: HR Strategy & Leadership
excerpt: Deciding between building an internal recruiting engine or partnering with an RPO? Here’s a practical rubric.
---
Scope, speed, specialization, and cost predict the optimal path. In hypergrowth or hard-to-fill scenarios, RPOs reduce time‑to‑fill while preserving quality through dedicated pipelines and talent intel. For predictable, high-volume roles, in‑house often wins on cost with a strong employer brand.
